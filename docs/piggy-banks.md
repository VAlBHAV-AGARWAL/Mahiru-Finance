# Piggy Banks (Savings Goals)

## Model

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `piggy_banks` | Goal: name, target_amount, current_amount, target_date, account link |
| `piggy_bank_events` | Activity: amount, date, linked journal |

## Features

1. **Create goal** — name, target amount, deadline, optional linked account
2. **Add funds** — contribute from a transaction (or manually)
3. **Withdraw** — take money out (cancels or reduces goal)
4. **Progress bar** — visual % complete with projected completion date
5. **Multiple goals** — separate savings for different purposes

## India Use Cases

| Goal | Typical Timeline |
|------|-----------------|
| Wedding fund | 2-5 years |
| Kids education | 5-15 years |
| House down payment | 3-7 years |
| Vacation | 6-12 months |
| Emergency fund | 6 months of expenses |
| New car | 1-3 years |
| Diwali shopping | 1 year |
