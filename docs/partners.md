# Partners & Business

For users who run a business with partners (very common in India — family businesses, proprietorships, small shops).

## Model

| Table | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| `partners` | Partner profile: name, profit_share_ratio, contact |
| `partner_ledger` | Capital contributions, drawings, profit/loss allocation |

## Features

1. **Add partner** — name, profit share ratio (%)
2. **Capital contribution** — partner invests money into business
3. **Drawings** — partner takes money out
4. **Profit allocation** — allocate period profit per ratio
5. **Ledger view** — running balance for each partner

## India Context

| Use Case | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Family business | Kirana store, restaurant, manufacturing |
| Freelance partnership | Design/development agency with co-founders |
| Professional firm | CA firm, law firm, medical practice |
| HUF | Hindu Undivided Family business |
